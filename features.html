Garden — Crystal Grid Canvas

Drag & Drop Grid Building

Tap a stone from the tray to place it on the canvas. Touch-hold and drag to reposition. Stones glow and animate as you move them.

Interactive

Snap-to-Grid & Symmetry

Toggle precision snapping. Cycle symmetry modes (2-fold, 3-fold, 6-fold) to mirror placements automatically.

Grid Replay

Chakra View

Visual body silhouette with 7 chakra points. Each shows related stones you can browse and use.
