1. Getting Started

Ishi no Niwa is a Japanese healing gemstone crystal grid app. You arrange sacred stones into geometric patterns, discover pairings, and build your spiritual practice.

1
Open the app link above on your phone
2
Tap one of the three Demo Login buttons (see below)
3
You'll land on the Garden tab — start placing stones!
All demo accounts have full access to every stone, template, and feature for testing purposes.
If the app looks stuck or shows an old version, do a hard refresh: pull down to reload on mobile, or Ctrl+Shift+R on desktop.

3. 庭 Garden

The main creative screen. Build crystal grids by placing gemstones onto a circular canvas.

How to Use

1
Choose a template — Tap the header text (e.g. "Choose a Template") to open the template picker. Scroll horizontally and tap a shape.
2
Place stones — Tap any stone in the bottom tray. It snaps to the next guide point on your template.
3
Drag to reposition — Press and drag any placed stone to move it around the canvas.
4
Add more — After filling all guide points, you can keep adding stones freely. They appear at center — drag them where you want.
5
Remove a stone — Tap a placed stone to select it (red ring appears). Hit the red Remove button. Tap Cancel to deselect.
6
Save — Tap "Save" in the top right. Earns +20 XP.

Toolbar Buttons

ButtonWhat it does
Snap Stones lock to a 20×20 invisible grid. Toggle on/off. Highlighted when active.
Symmetry Cycles: Off → 2-fold → 3-fold → 6-fold. When active, adding a stone places rotational copies around center. Shows fold number (e.g. "Symmetry:3").
Sound Toggle for ambient sounds (visual toggle only in this version).
Energize Pulses all placed stones 3 times. Works whenever stones are on the canvas. This is the "activate the grid" ritual step.
Clear Removes all stones and resets the template.
Connection lines automatically appear between all placed stones, creating the geometric network pattern.

5. 導 Guide

An assisted grid-building flow. The guide recommends stones and shapes based on your intention.

3-Step Flow

1
Pick an intention — 8 options: Love, Prosperity, Healing, Protection, Spiritual, Calm, Career, Courage
2
Pick a shape — A horizontal list of geometric shapes appears, filtered to match your intention. Each shows a glyph, name, and point count.
3
See recommendation — A center stone and supporting stones are suggested. Then choose:
Place for Me Auto-places stones onto the template and opens the Garden (+30 XP)
I'll Customize Opens the Garden with an empty template so you arrange them yourself
The guide is a starting point. Once in the Garden, you can always add more stones, remove stones, or rearrange everything.

7. 私 Profile

  • Avatar — Shows your avatar gemstone and level badge
  • Subscription — Current plan display. Upgrade button for Free tier.
  • Language — Tap to toggle between English and Japanese (日本語). The entire app switches instantly.

Sign Out erases all local progress. On next login, demo accounts start fresh with full unlocks.

Profile

  • App is responsive on your device screen size
