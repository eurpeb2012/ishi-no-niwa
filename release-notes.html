New Grid Templates
Two new templates inspired by real crystal mandala patterns:

New
Improved Photo Mode
Full-screen clean view with no circle border — your grid fills the screen like a real photograph. Back button (top-left) and Share button (bottom-right corner) stay subtle and out of the way. Also uses your chosen canvas background.
Improved
Fairy Removed from Garden
The fairy companion avatar has been moved off the grid canvas — it now appears only on your Profile page, keeping the garden clean and focused on your crystal arrangement.
Removed from Garden

📖Stones Guide

Crystal of the Day — Now Explains Why
The daily crystal recommendation now includes a full explanation: current moon phase, its energy description, and how the recommended stone harmonizes with today's lunar cycle. Example:

"Today's Waxing Crescent 🌒 brings prosperity & courage energy. Energy is building. Focus on growth and take the first steps toward your goals. This stone harmonizes with today's lunar cycle."
Improved

👤Profile

Grid Wallpaper
Set any saved grid as your profile card background! A wallpaper picker appears with thumbnail previews of all your saved grids. Tap to set, tap again to remove. Your crystal fairy and profile info float above the wallpaper.
