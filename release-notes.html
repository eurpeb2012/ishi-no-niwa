Ishi no Niwa — Crystal Garden

💎Crystal Rarity System (#2)

4-Tier Crystal Rarity
Crystals now have real-world based rarity tiers: Common (Rose Quartz, Amethyst), Uncommon (Citrine, Moonstone), Rare (Labradorite, Sugilite), and Legendary / Gem Quality (Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald). Rarer crystals provide higher energy multipliers when used in grids.
New

👗Avatar Outfit Customization (#3)

Watercolor Kawaii Fairy Redesign
Fairy avatar completely redesigned to match Takae's concept art — soft watercolor style with translucent wings, flowing hair, chibi proportions, and blush cheeks. 4 color variants: Rose Quartz (pink), Amethyst (purple), Peridot (green), Onyx (navy).
Redesigned
Outfit Customization System
Equip different wings, dresses, crowns, and accessories on your fairy. Outfits unlock via evolution stages, quest rewards, or in-app purchase. 15 unique outfits including Sakura Wings, Moonlight Gown, Crystal Tiara, and Guardian Wings.
New
Wing Shape Variations
5 wing shapes: Round (basic), Petal (Bloom Fairy), Butterfly (purchasable), Crystal (stage 4), and Feather (Guardian). Each has unique proportions.
New

🏰Fairy Land World Building (#4)

5 Biomes to Explore
Crystal Meadow → Crystal Garden → Enchanted Forest → Crystal Cave → Sky Realm. Each biome unlocks at higher energy thresholds. Build structures using crystal energy earned from creating grids.
New
18 Structures to Build
From a cozy Fairy House to a Crystal Aquarium to a floating Cloud Palace. Structures include: Crystal Lamp, Flower Patch, Crystal Fountain, Rose Quartz Arbor, Butterfly Grove, Fairy Ring, Crystal Pond, Amethyst Cluster, Gem Throne, Rainbow Bridge, Star Garden, and more.
New

🏪Crystal Shop Partnerships (#5)

Partner Crystal Shops
Browse featured crystal shops in the profile section. Partners include Shibuya Crystal Gallery, Healing Stones Japan, Crystal Mineral Market, Gem Palace Kyoto, and Earth Crystal Online. Each shop shows their specialty crystals and location.

⚡Crystal Energy System (#12)

Grids Generate Energy
Every grid you save now generates Crystal Energy (結晶エネルギー) — 5 base + 2 per crystal placed. Energy also comes from completing quests. Energy powers your fairy's evolution and is displayed on your Journey and Profile screens alongside your bond level.
New

🖼️Profile Wallpaper Controls (#9)

Size & Angle Adjustments
Profile wallpaper now has sizing controls: scale from 50% to 250%, rotate in 15-degree increments, and a Reset button to return to defaults. The wallpaper transforms are applied to the grid display behind your profile card.
New
